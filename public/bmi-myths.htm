BMI Myths

There are many myths about BMI. Learn the facts and understand what BMI really indicates about your health.

Common Myths About BMI

  • BMI is an accurate measure of health for everyone.
  • All body fat is bad and needs to be reduced.
  • BMI accounts for muscle mass.
  • If you have a normal BMI, you are completely healthy.
  • BMI is the same for men, women, and children.
  • A higher BMI always means higher health risks.
