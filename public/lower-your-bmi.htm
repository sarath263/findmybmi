How to Lower BMI

Lowering your BMI can be achieved through a combination of healthy eating, regular physical activity, and lifestyle changes.

  • Adopt a balanced and nutritious diet
  • Increase physical activity and exercise regularly
  • Monitor portion sizes and practice mindful eating
  • Drink plenty of water and limit sugary beverages
  • Incorporate more fruits and vegetables into meals
  • Reduce consumption of processed and high-fat foods
  • Get an adequate amount of sleep each night
  • Seek support from a healthcare professional or nutritionist
