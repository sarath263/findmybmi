Health Risks of High BMI

High BMI is associated with several health risks including cardiovascular diseases, diabetes, and certain cancers.

Certainly:
  • Increased risk of cardiovascular diseases
  • Type 2 diabetes
  • Hypertension (high blood pressure)
  • Stroke
  • Certain types of cancer
  • Sleep apnea
  • Osteoarthritis
  • Fatty liver disease
